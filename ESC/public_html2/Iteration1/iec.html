Intercollegiate Engineering Conference


When Saturday, Nov. 5th, 10 am to Sunday, Nov. 6th, 1 pm
Where Bechtel Engineering Center, Berkeley, CA, United States Berkeley, CA
The inaugural Intercollegiate Engineering Conference is a 2-day event that aims to bring together engineering clubs and societies from around California in a collaborative, collective event. Attending students will have opportunities to communicate with clubs across different campuses, build strong relationships with industry sponsors via engaging workshops and keynotes, learn key take- aways for improvements of their own clubs, and present their clubsâ€™ projects and progress over the year. By engaging in activities that emphasize professional development and social growth, students will have the opportunity to learn from each other, and use what they have learned to catalyze change on their local campuses and communities.

Corporate sponsors will also have the exclusive opportunity to reach out and interact with a large number of diverse students spanning various colleges with ease at one location. Additionally, the Turn the Tables Fair is a unique event where sponsors can learn about potential partnerships with students organizations.
