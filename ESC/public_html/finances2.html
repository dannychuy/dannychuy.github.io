How do I use ESC funds?

If your student group received funding from ESC, please read to file a purchase (reimbursement) request for the items purchased:
NOTE: Funds are not given beforehand; they are reimbursed after purchase.

To fill out a purchase request form, go to ESC's Callink and click on the Finance tab. The Finance tab is visible to your student group's treasurer and/or president if they attended treasurer training at the beginning of the semester, which is required. Fill out the form, make sure to click the correct account (Misc or Programs [ASUCFunds] depending on what you're asking to be reimbursed for). Turn in the form along with the stapled original receipt(s) to the ESS Office front desk in Bechtel.

The ESC Treasurers will take care of the rest and notify you if there's any issues. Make sure to take a picture of the original receipt(s) in case something happens to the receipts. Have the purchase request number noted somewhere to check it's status on Callink under the finance tab. Optional: Email the ESC Treasurers to notify them that you've turned in form(s) to the ESS.

More information is on the treasurer training slides that we sent out to those who attended treasurer training. Additionally, the difference between Misc and Programs funds is on those slides.
