E98: The Insider's Guide to Berkeley Engineering

Engineering 98 is a student-run seminar (DeCal) designed and taught by Berkeley engineering upperclassmen. The course covers everything an incoming engineering student needs to know in a casual class setting. Our goal is to help every incoming engineer gain the wisdom of an upper-division student while making long-lasting connections with upperclassmen and fellow students.

Each section consists of 2 instructors at 20 students - check out our staff to see which section is your best fit!

Feel free to contact us, the E98 Coordinators, at e98coords@gmail.com.

Enrollment Procedures:

Each section is capped at 20 seats and based on first-come, first-serve basis. Students who register in full sections will be placed on a waitlist.
Enroll in Engineering 98 like you would with any other class - on CalCentral! On CalCentral's My Academics tab,
- Under "4. Class Enrollment", click on "Choose classes".
- You will be directed to a new page. Click "Search".
- Set Subject as "Engineering" and Course Number as "is exactly" and "98".
- Click on "Search" and choose from Sections 003 to 012 or 014! (Note: do NOT enroll in Section 001 or 002.)
There are zero resources for this class, goodluck!
